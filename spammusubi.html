Spam Musubi

Get Ready! This is an actual recipe and it is bomb. Spam Musubis are everywhere in Hawai'i and you can get it anywhere, specifically in every single 7-11. They are delicious, easy to eat and carry with you on the go. Spam Musubi are simple to make and if you are not like me and you do not have a spam musubi maker, then use the can it came it and wrap it in plastic wrap. 😊

Ingredients

Main Ingredients Sauce
  • 1 can Spam
  • Seaweed
  • Sushi Rice
  • 1/4 cup of Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup of White Sugar
  • 1 tbsp of Mirin

Instructions

  1. Cut spam in 1 cm wide slices.
  2. On a medium hot pan, cook the spam.
  3. Mix the sauce ingredients together until the sugar dissolves.
  4. Flip the spam when the it turns a nice brown color.
  5. After a few minutes, add in the sauce to the pan. Lower the heat, so the sauce can slowly thicken.
  6. Flip the spam again.
  7. Transfer the spam onto a plate.
  8. Set up your station with your musubi maker in front, your rice and spam on the side.
  9. Put rice in the maker and compress.
  10. Push out and lay a piece of spam onto.
  11. Then wrap it all in your piece of seaweed cut down to its proper size.
  12. Eat and Throughly Enjoy.
