Torta Talong

A filipino dish that my mom makes for me. Besides being pan-fried on oil, it is a healthy meal with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Asian Eggplant
  • Eggs
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt or Garlic Salt

Instructions

  1. Bake, Roast, or Steam the asian eggplant.
    Roasting it best. Steaming is simpler.
  2. When cooled down, peel the eggplant.
  3. Smash the eggplant like a thin pancake.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix egg and salt.
  5. Coat the eggplant in the egg mixture.
  6. In a medium hot pan, heat add in some oil.
  7. When the oil is hot, cook the eggplant.
  8. After a couple minutes, flip.
  9. Eat and enjoy with a side of rice and some soy sauce.
