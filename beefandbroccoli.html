Beef and Broccoli



My favorite beef and broccoli recipe. I found it online and it has been my go-to.

I make this after a dinner of Korean Barbeque with the leftover beef brisket. When I have Korean BBQ at home, I cut the meat myself with a meat slicer and frozen meat blocks.


Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 lb of ribeye steak
  • Some Broccoli
  • A Little Bit of Carrots
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Cooking Wine
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds
  1. Cut the ribeye in thin slices
  2. Create the marinade
  3. Let the ribeye sit in the marinade for at least half an hour
  4. Prepare the vegetables and other ingredients
  5. Boil the broccoli
  6. Cook the ribeye on high for 2 minutes & Set the meat aside
  7. Cook the onions, garlic, and ginger
  8. Add the meat back in and mix
  9. Add the sauce
  10. Mix until combined
  11. Add sesame oil and then serve
